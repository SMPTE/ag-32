This Administrative Guideline provides guidelines on how Artificial Intelligence (AI) can be used in the development of SMPTE standards. It is intended to help TC members understand how AI can be used to assist in the development of standards, and to provide guidance on how to use AI in a way that is consistent with the principles of standardization.
